Smart Matching Algorithm

Our AI matches borrowers with lenders based on location, item requirements, rental history, and compatibility scores.

  • Location-Based Matching: Finds items available in your hostel/campus area
  • Trust Scoring: AI calculates reliability scores for safe transactions
  • Schedule Optimization: Suggests optimal pickup/return times
  • Price Suggestions: Recommends fair rental prices based on demand
